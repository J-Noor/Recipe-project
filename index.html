Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper
  • 1 tbsp butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables,cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs
  2. Heat the pan
  3. Cook the omlette
  4. Add fillings
  5. Fold and serve
  6. Enjoy
